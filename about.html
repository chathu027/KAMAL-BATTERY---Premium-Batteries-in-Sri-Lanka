About Us

Your trusted battery specialists since 2010

Our Story

Founded in 1997 , Kamal Battery began as a business with a passion for providing quality power solutions to our community. What started as a modest storefront has grown into one of the most trusted battery specialists in Sri Lanka.

Over the years, we've expanded our product range and services, but our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction remains unchanged. We take pride in offering only the best products from reputable brands, backed by our expert knowledge and exceptional service.

Today, we serve thousands of satisfied customers across the island, from individual vehicle owners to large commercial operations. Our team of certified technicians continues to uphold the highest standards in battery installation, maintenance, and customer care.
